About Us

At Trykk and Trad, we believe that every brand has a story — and we help you tell it through bold prints, unique designs, and professional marketing materials. Whether it's a custom t-shirt, an eye-catching logo, or impactful business stationery, we ensure every detail reflects your vision.

With a passion for quality and creativity, we serve businesses of all sizes with tailor-made solutions in Apparel Customization, Graphic Design, Offset & Digital Printing, and Digital Branding. Our team is committed to turning your ideas into reality — with style, speed, and satisfaction.

Our Mission

To empower businesses, brands, and individuals by delivering high-quality, creative, and affordable design, print, and digital solutions – all with a commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To become a trusted and leading creative hub in India for apparel printing, branding, and digital services — known for our creativity, quality, and excellence in customer experience.

Our Core Values

We believe in creativity, customer satisfaction, consistent quality, timely delivery, and continuous innovation to bring your ideas to life.
